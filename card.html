Erinaceus

Erinaceus is a genus of hedgehog from the family of Erinaceidae. There are four main species of Erinaceus.

  • Size: 20–30 cm
  • Weight: 400–1200 g
  • average life span : 2–5 years
  • diet: omnivore

Erinaceus is like all hedgehogs in that the major physical characteristic is the spines. These spines are a modification of the hair that is formed and strengthened by keratin. The spines contain hollow air filled gaps separated by thin inner layers of the spine to lessen the weight load.
